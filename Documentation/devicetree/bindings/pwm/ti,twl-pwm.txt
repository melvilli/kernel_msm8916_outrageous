Texas Instruments TWL series PWM drivers

Supported PWMs:
On TWL4030 series: PWM1 and PWM2
On TWL6030 series: PWM0 and PWM1

Required properties:
- compatible: "ti,twl4030-pwm" or "ti,twl6030-pwm"
